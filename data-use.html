How Brikk Uses Your Data

Transparency in data handling is core to our mission.

✓ We route requests to the providers you choose using your keys

Your provider credentials are encrypted at rest and only used to fulfill your API requests. We never share them with third parties.

✓ We store immutable logs so you can audit performance, cost, and errors

Every request is logged with metadata (provider, latency, tokens, cost, status). You control retention via your plan. Export is available on supported tiers.

✓ We do not train models on your content

Your inputs and outputs remain yours. We process them only to provide routing, logging, and analytics. We never use your data to train AI models.

✓ Provider credentials are encrypted at rest and never logged

We use AES-256 encryption for all provider API keys. They're never written to logs, never sent to third parties, and only decrypted in memory when needed to route your requests.

✓ You control retention via your plan

Free: 7 days • Hacker: 30 days • Starter: 90 days • Professional: 365 days. Export is available on Starter and Professional tiers.

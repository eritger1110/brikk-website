Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 5, 2025

Who we are

Brikk ("we", "us") provides an AI routing and orchestration platform at app.getbrikk.com and api.getbrikk.com.

What we collect

  • Account & Org info: name, email, organization, role.
  • Usage & telemetry: timestamps, latency, errors, models/providers used, token counts, approximate cost.
  • Immutable request logs (customer-controlled): request/response metadata and optional audit_context you send us.
  • Provider credentials (BYO keys): stored encrypted; never logged in plaintext.
  • Website analytics: pseudonymous analytics and cookie data to improve the service.

What we don't do

  • We do not sell personal data.
  • We do not train models on your request content or logs.
  • We do not access your providers except to fulfill routing you initiate.

How we use data

  • Provide and improve the service (routing, fallback, flows, logs).
  • Security, fraud prevention, support, and compliance.
  • Product analytics (aggregate/anonymous).

Data retention

  • Log storage is tier-based by call volume (last N calls) or by policy; see your plan details in the dashboard.
  • You can export logs (CSV/S3 on supported tiers).
  • We purge data beyond the plan's retention.

Subprocessors

  • Hosting/infra: Amazon Web Services (compute, network, storage).
  • Auth: Auth0 (identity).
  • Email: Amazon SES (transactional).

(We will update this list as needed.)

Security

  • TLS 1.3 in transit; AES-256 at rest for provider credentials; API keys hashed (scrypt/argon2).
  • Principle of least privilege; secrets never logged.

Your choices

  • Access, update, or delete your account data via the app or by emailing support@getbrikk.com.
  • Opt out of non-essential cookies via the cookie banner.

International transfers

We may process data in the United States. Appropriate safeguards are applied.

Children

The service is not for individuals under 16.

Contact

privacy@getbrikk.com, security@getbrikk.com

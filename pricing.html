Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a credit card for the Free plan?

Yes, we require a credit card for all plans, including Free. This helps prevent abuse and ensures you can seamlessly upgrade when you're ready to scale.

What happens if I exceed my plan limits?

We'll notify you when you approach your limits. You can either upgrade to a higher plan or your agents will be temporarily throttled until the next billing cycle.

Can I change plans anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately with prorated billing.

What's included in support?

Free plans get community support, paid plans get email support, and Professional plans get dedicated support with guaranteed response times.
